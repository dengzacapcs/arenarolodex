Lowell Arena Helper

Instructions:

Please enter all of your classes below. Do not worry about free blocks, they are accounted for at the end. If you prefer a certain free block, you can enter it at the bottom.

How sorting works

This tool calculates every single possible course combination you can take, and sorts it by best to worst. Each schedule gets +1 pref points for each preferred teacher, and +1 points for each preferred block. Schedules are displayed from highest preferability score to lowest.
